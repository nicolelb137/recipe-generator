### **One-Pan Chicken and Veggies Recipe**

**Ingredients:**
- 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (or thighs)
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 cup baby carrots
- 1 cup broccoli florets
- 1 cup diced potatoes (or use sweet potatoes for a healthier option)
- Fresh parsley (optional, for garnish)

**Instructions:**
1. **Preheat the oven** to 400째F (200째C).
2. **Prepare the chicken**: Pat the chicken breasts dry with paper towels. Rub them with 1 tablespoon of olive oil and season with garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, paprika, salt, and pepper on both sides.
3. **Prepare the veggies**: In a large bowl, toss the baby carrots, broccoli florets, and diced potatoes with the remaining 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Season with a little salt and pepper.
4. **Assemble**: On a baking sheet, place the chicken in the center, and arrange the veggies around the chicken.
5. **Bake**: Place the pan in the oven and bake for about 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165째F (74째C) and the vegetables are tender.
6. **Serve**: Remove from the oven and garnish with fresh parsley if desired. Serve everything directly from the pan for easy cleanup.

**Tips:**
- You can use any vegetables you like (e.g., zucchini, bell peppers, or asparagus).
- Add a squeeze of lemon juice over the chicken and veggies for a fresh touch after baking.
